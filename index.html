I am a programmer, teacher, and font enthusiast in New York City.

My favorite language is Kotlin, my most used language is Python, but the best language is JavaScript.

I graduated Stony Brook University in 2019 with a B.S. in Computer Science. You can find a lot of my programming projects around the internet. Here on my site and a few other places.

Generally, my favorite fonts are the grotesque and neo-grotesque sans-serifs. Born of clean, neutral Swiss and industrial German design, respectively, fonts like Helvetica, Akzidenz-Grotesk, and Univers are some of my favorite. Other big highlights include Menlo, Monaco, Hoefler Text, Minion Pro, Frutiger, Avenir, Source Code Pro, and the IBM Plex family. There's many other great ones, but I do have a softspot for these.
